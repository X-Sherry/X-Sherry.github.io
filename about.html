A bit about me

Hi, this is Sherry:) I'm a junior at the University of Pennsylvania pursuing a bachelor's degree in Systems Engineering with a concentration in AI and Data Science and a accelerated master's degree in Systems Engineering.

Driven by curiosity and a passion for solving complex problems, I'm fascinated by the intersection of business, technology, and strategy. I enjoy breaking down ambiguous challenges into clear, actionable solutions. Whether I'm exploring new industries, studying abroad, or building new skills, I'm always looking for opportunities to learn, adapt, and create meaningful impact.

When I'm not working on a project, I'm probably planning my next trip, trying out a new café, listening to music in a language I don't speak, or convincing myself that going to the gym counts as a hobby (it does). I love meeting people with different perspectives and believe some of the best lessons come from experiences outside the classroom. Those moments have made me more adaptable, open-minded, and excited to keep learning.
